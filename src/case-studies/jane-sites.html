The_Situation

A legacy multi-tenant Ruby on Rails asset remained an underutilized business unit due to high technical debt and a manual onboarding process that required hours of specialized labor per clinic.

The objective was to transform this idle asset into a Market-Ready MVP by refactoring core infrastructure, hardening security protocols, and engineering a self-serve provisioning engine to facilitate rapid customer acquisition.

Implementation

01. Provisioning & Onboarding Automation

Architected a "Product-Led Growth" onboarding flow that automated clinic instance setup. This eliminated the manual intervention bottleneck, reducing Time-to-Value (TTV) for new practitioners by 95%.

AUTO_PROVISION TENANT_ISOLATION

02. Infrastructure Hardening

Remedied critical vulnerabilities in the legacy file ingestion system. Deployed a hardened storage abstraction layer ensuring PIPEDA-compliant data residency and encrypted S3 bucket isolation for clinic assets.

Verified Outcome

Modernized the platform to achieve Product-Market Fit, leading to immediate customer acquisition and a successful exit to Jane.app.
