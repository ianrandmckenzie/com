## Strategic Content & Search Protocol: `ianrandmckenzie.com`

This document defines the content architecture and search engine positioning for the **Senior Solutions Architect** persona. The objective is to secure high-value FTE roles ($140k+ CAD) while maintaining a $1,800/mo **Industrial SEO Advisory** pipeline.

---

### 1. The Three-Track Content Taxonomy
Content must be strictly categorized into the established color-coded tracks. Any content falling outside these parameters is discarded to prevent "narrative drift."

| Track | Category | Strategic Objective | Content Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blue** | **Architecture** | High-Value Recruitment | Case studies, system design patterns, compliance frameworks (SOC2/HIPAA). |
| **Green** | **Advisory** | Income Stability | Industrial SEO audits, revenue-engine mechanics, regional exclusivity reports. |
| **Purple** | **Labs** | Technical Authority | Rust/Ruby performance benchmarks, memory-safety research, algorithmic visualizations. |

---

### 2. Search Engine Positioning (SEP)
The search strategy is **Defensive** for the Primary Identity and **Aggressive** for the Advisory Income.

#### A. Authority Signal (The "Architect" Keywords)
Target low-volume, high-intent technical strings to attract CTOs and Head of Engineering profiles.
* **Keywords:** `Solutions Architect Rust Ruby`, `Zero-Trust Infrastructure Canada`, `Legacy System Migration Specialist`, `HIPAA Compliant SaaS Architecture`.
* **Tactical Execution:** Blog posts should function as **Technical Whitepapers**. Avoid "How-to" guides; focus on "Why/How I Architected [X] for [Y] Outcome."

#### B. Revenue Engine (The "Industrial" Keywords)
Target regional, high-leverage industrial terms to feed the Green Track.
* **Keywords:** `Industrial Equipment SEO Advisory`, `Heavy Rental Revenue Optimization`, `Inventory Catalog Search Performance`.
* **Tactical Execution:** Use the "Revenue Safety Net" narrative. Focus on **Market Dominance** and **Exclusivity**.

---

### 3. Editorial Guardrails (Voice & Tone)
Adherence to the **Rigid** persona is mandatory for all long-form content.

* **Prohibited Phrasing:** "I feel," "Basically," "In my opinion," "Passionate about," "Excited to share."
* **Mandatory Verbs:** *Provisioned, Hardened, Orchestrated, Mitigated, Optimized, Leveraged.*
* **Structure:**
    1.  **The Constraint:** Define the technical or business bottleneck.
    2.  **The Implementation:** Detail the architecture (Code/Infrastructure).
    3.  **The Outcome:** Document the fiscal or operational impact ($10M+ impact logic).

---

### 4. Technical SEO Specifications
All blog implementations must prioritize sub-second performance to validate the "Technical Advisor" claim.

* **Stack:** Vanilla HTML/Tailwind/Markdown. No heavy CMS (WordPress/Ghost).
* **Performance:** Maintain 100/100 Lighthouse scores.
* **Metadata:** Every post must include `font-mono` technical headers (e.g., `REF_ID`, `LOAD_TIME`, `RELEVANCE_SCORE`).
* **Canonicalization:** `ianrandmckenzie.com` is the global canonical root. Any cross-posts or legacy references must point here.

---

### 5. Content-to-Revenue Mapping
Every 4 blog posts should follow this distribution to maintain the pivot balance:

1.  **Post 1 (Blue):** Deep-dive into a specific compliance or scaling challenge.
2.  **Post 2 (Green):** Analysis of "Technical Leakage" in industrial search results.
3.  **Post 3 (Blue/Purple):** A systems-level benchmark (e.g., Rust vs. Ruby for concurrent ingestion).
4.  **Post 4 (Green):** A "State of the Region" advisory note on industrial digital infrastructure.
