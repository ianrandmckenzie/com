# System Definition Document: I.R.M. Professional Portfolio (2026)

# 1. Executive Persona & Identity
Identity: Ian R. McKenzie.

Role: Senior Solutions Architect / Technical Advisor.

Target Market: High-stakes enterprise environments ($140k+ CAD) in Canada & USA.

The Persona: "The Pragmatic Expert." The tone is clinical, authoritative, and precise. It reflects a former founder who has successfully transitioned from "startup chaos" to "enterprise stability".

Core Value Proposition: A rare combination of US/Canada Dual Citizenship, Security Clearance eligibility, and over $10M in documented business impact.

# 2. Technical Communication Protocol (The "Rigid" Rule)
Verbs of Authority: Use industrial-strength action verbs: Architected, Provisioned, Mitigated, Optimized, Standardized, Orchestrated, Audited.

Negative Constraints: * NO startup jargon: "Crushing it," "disrupting," "passionate," "hustle," or "rockstar".

NO casual conversational filler: "I feel like," "Basically," or "I just wanted to."

NO unverified claims: Every technical feature must be tethered to a business metric (Revenue, Performance, or Compliance).

The Filter: All creative or "hobby" projects must be reframed as Applied Research or Systems Benchmarking.

# 3. Design Language & Visual System (TailwindCSS)
Color Palette:

Base: bg-slate-900 (Deep professional blue-gray).

Detail Inset: bg-slate-950 (Used for technical data cards and sidebars).

Accents: Blue (text-blue-500) for Architecture (Blue track); Green (text-green-500) for Advisory/Industrial SEO (Green track); Purple (text-purple-500) for Research/Labs (Purple track). These map directly to the Three-Track Content Taxonomy defined in content_strategy.md. Green is NOT used for compliance content — HIPAA/SOC2/PIPEDA content belongs to the Blue (Architecture) track.

Visual Motifs:

High Density: Use information-rich grids rather than expansive "breathable" white space.

Structured Borders: Use border-slate-800 to create a "contained" and professional aesthetic.

Status Indicators: Use font-mono and small "pulsing" green indicators to signify live systems or active credentials.
